The F# Open Engineering Technical Group

The F# Open Engineering Group is a working group associated with The F# Software Foundation, an organization dedicated to F#, including cross-platform and open-source F#. The F# Open Engineering Group's responsibilities include The Open Edition of F# and an affiliated collection of community projects at GitHub. Help us bring F# to your favorite platforms, and help us empower F# users on all platforms.

Open Engineering Group

The F# Open Engineering group manages the open-source F# Compiler and Components repository as well as the F# Community Project Incubation Space. The group also helps to facilitate open-source contributions to the Visual F# Language and Tools.

To contribute and get involved with the group activities:

  • Join our Google Group
  • Get the Open Edition of F#
  • Contribute to our repositories
  • Contribute to incubation projects
  • Get involved in the Mono project
